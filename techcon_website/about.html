History

TechCon was founded in 2010 with a vision to bring together technology enthusiasts, innovators, and industry leaders from around the globe. What started as a small gathering of 200 attendees has grown into one of the most prestigious technology conferences, now hosting over 5,000 participants annually.

Over the years, TechCon has been at the forefront of technological innovation, showcasing groundbreaking developments in artificial intelligence, blockchain, cloud computing, and cybersecurity. Each year builds upon the success of the previous one, creating a legacy of knowledge sharing and networking.

Mission

Our mission is to empower technology professionals and enthusiasts by providing a platform for learning, collaboration, and innovation. We strive to:

  • Foster connections between industry leaders and emerging talent
  • Showcase cutting-edge technologies and their real-world applications
  • Promote diversity and inclusion in the tech industry
  • Inspire the next generation of innovators and problem solvers

TechCon is committed to creating an environment where ideas flourish and partnerships are formed, driving the technology industry forward.

Past Speakers

TechCon has been honored to host some of the most influential voices in technology. Here are just a few of our notable past speakers:

Dr. Sarah Chen

Dr. Chen is a leading artificial intelligence researcher and the founder of AI Ethics Institute. At TechCon 2023, she delivered a groundbreaking keynote on responsible AI development and the future of machine learning in healthcare.

Marcus Rodriguez

As the Chief Security Officer of CyberShield Technologies, Marcus has been instrumental in developing next-generation security protocols. His 2022 presentation on quantum encryption captivated audiences and sparked important industry conversations.

Jennifer Park

Jennifer is a cloud computing pioneer and the CTO of CloudScale Solutions. Her TechCon 2021 workshop on serverless architecture and microservices has influenced countless development teams worldwide.
